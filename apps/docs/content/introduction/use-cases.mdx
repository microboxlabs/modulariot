# Use Cases

ModularIoT supports diverse operational scenarios across industries.

## Fleet Management

Monitor vehicle fleets in real-time:

- Location tracking and geofencing
- Driver behavior analysis
- Fuel efficiency monitoring
- Maintenance scheduling

## Asset Tracking

Track and manage distributed assets:

- Equipment utilization monitoring
- Theft and unauthorized use detection
- Environmental condition tracking
- Compliance reporting

## Logistics Operations

Optimize delivery and supply chain:

- Real-time shipment visibility
- Temperature-sensitive cargo monitoring
- Route optimization support
- Delivery confirmation

## Field Service

Support mobile workforce operations:

- Technician dispatch optimization
- Job completion tracking
- Vehicle and equipment status
- Safety compliance

## Industrial Equipment

Monitor industrial machinery:

- Predictive maintenance
- Operational efficiency tracking
- Safety threshold monitoring
- Regulatory compliance
