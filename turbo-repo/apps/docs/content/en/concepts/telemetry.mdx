# Telemetry

Telemetry is the raw data transmitted by devices to the platform. Understanding telemetry is foundational to working with ModularIoT.

## What is Telemetry?

Telemetry refers to the automated collection and transmission of data from remote devices. In ModularIoT, telemetry encompasses:

- **Location data** - GPS coordinates, speed, heading
- **Sensor readings** - Temperature, pressure, voltage
- **Vehicle data** - Engine parameters, fuel levels, diagnostics
- **Events** - Ignition, door opens, threshold crossings

## Telemetry vs Metrics

**Telemetry** is raw, device-specific data in the format sent by the device.

**Metrics** are normalized, canonical representations extracted from telemetry.

The platform transforms telemetry into metrics through drivers.

## Characteristics of Telemetry

- **Time-stamped** - Every reading has an associated time
- **Device-specific** - Format depends on manufacturer
- **Variable quality** - May contain gaps, delays, or errors
- **High volume** - Devices often report frequently
