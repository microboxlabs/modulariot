# Symptoms Engine

The Symptoms Engine is the intelligence layer of ModularIoT, transforming raw telemetry into actionable operational insights through real-time detection and state management.

## Purpose

The engine continuously evaluates incoming telemetry against defined rules and conditions, detecting symptoms as they emerge and tracking them through their full lifecycle.

## Core Capabilities

- **Detection** - Rule-based and pattern-based symptom identification
- **Stateful Symptoms** - Maintaining symptom state across time
- **Severity Escalation** - Automatic severity adjustments based on duration or conditions
- **Persistence** - Durable symptom state for historical analysis
- **Workflow Integration** - Connecting symptoms to operator actions

## Architecture

The engine operates as a stateful stream processor, maintaining symptom state in-memory while persisting checkpoints for recovery and historical queries.
