# Delivery Compliance Story Skill

## Goal

Explain delivery compliance for a selected period, identify the operational
bottleneck, and propose one dashboard widget draft.

## Required Evidence

- Delivery compliance rate.
- Change against previous comparable period.
- Late delivery count.
- Top workflow bottleneck.
- Number or share of affected deliveries.

## Narrative Sections

1. What changed.
2. Where the operation slows down.
3. Recommended dashboard addition.

## Safety

Dashboard patches are drafts until approved by the user. Never apply a patch
without an approval id.
