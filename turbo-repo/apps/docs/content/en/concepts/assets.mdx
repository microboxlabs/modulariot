# Assets

Assets are the physical or logical entities that ModularIoT monitors.

## What is an Asset?

An asset represents something valuable that you want to track and monitor:

- **Vehicles** - Cars, trucks, trailers, equipment
- **Equipment** - Generators, compressors, tools
- **Containers** - Shipping containers, cargo
- **Infrastructure** - Facilities, stations, sites

## Assets vs Devices

**Devices** are hardware that produces telemetry (GPS trackers, sensors).

**Assets** are the things being monitored (vehicles, equipment).

A vehicle (asset) might have multiple devices attached: a GPS tracker, an OBD reader, and a temperature sensor.

## Asset Properties

- **Identity** - Unique identifier and name
- **Type** - Classification for grouping and rules
- **Ownership** - Tenant and organizational assignment
- **Metadata** - Additional attributes (make, model, year)

## Asset Lifecycle

Assets are:
1. **Registered** - Added to the platform
2. **Associated** - Linked to monitoring devices
3. **Monitored** - Receiving telemetry
4. **Retired** - Removed from active monitoring
