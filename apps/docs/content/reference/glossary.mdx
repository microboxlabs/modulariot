# Glossary

Definitions of key terms used throughout ModularIoT documentation.

## A

**Asset**
A physical or logical entity being monitored (vehicle, equipment, container).

**API Key**
Credential for authenticating API requests, scoped to a tenant.

## C

**Canonical Key**
Standardized metric name used across the platform (e.g., `position.latitude`).

**Control Tower**
The operational dashboard for fleet management and symptom response.

## D

**Device**
Hardware that produces telemetry data (GPS tracker, sensor, OBD reader).

**Driver**
Software component that translates device-specific protocols to canonical format.

## E

**Event**
A discrete occurrence at a specific moment in time.

## I

**Ingestion**
The process of receiving and accepting telemetry data into the platform.

## M

**Metric**
A canonical, normalized measurement with defined semantics.

## S

**SDK**
Software Development Kit - client libraries for integrating with ModularIoT.

**Severity**
Classification of symptom urgency (info, warning, critical, emergency).

**Symptom**
A detected condition that may require attention or action.

## T

**Telemetry**
Raw data transmitted by devices to the platform.

**Tenant**
Top-level organizational unit with complete data isolation.

## W

**Worklist**
Prioritized queue of symptoms for operator attention.
