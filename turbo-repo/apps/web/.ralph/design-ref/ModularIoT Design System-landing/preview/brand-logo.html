logo.svg and logo2.svg are the real ModularIoT marks — a navy/teal mountain glyph with the "ModularIoT" wordmark. These live at the platform layer and ship on every implementation's admin, docs, and OSS surfaces. Mintral (yellow "M" + slate wordmark) is a whitelabel example — one of many tenant skins that swap into the platform's logo slot. New tenants drop their mark into assets/tenants/<tenant>/logo.* and inherit the same slot dimensions, padding, and clear-space rules from the platform.
