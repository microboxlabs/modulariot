# Prioritization

How symptoms are ordered and surfaced for operator attention.

## Priority Factors

Symptoms are prioritized based on:
- **Severity** - Critical before warning before info
- **Age** - Older unresolved symptoms escalate
- **Asset importance** - VIP assets prioritized
- **Symptom type** - Safety over operational

## Priority Score

Each symptom receives a calculated priority score:
- Higher score = higher priority
- Score updates as conditions change
- Visible in worklists

## Automatic Prioritization

The platform automatically:
- Sorts worklists by priority
- Highlights urgent items
- Surfaces escalating symptoms
- Groups related symptoms

## Manual Override

Operators can:
- Pin important symptoms
- Defer non-urgent items
- Escalate manually
- Set priority holds

## Prioritization Rules

Configure prioritization for your operations:
- Weight factors
- Custom scoring rules
- Business hour adjustments
- Holiday schedules

## Best Practices

- Work highest priority first
- Don't skip without reason
- Document deferrals
- Review deferred items regularly
