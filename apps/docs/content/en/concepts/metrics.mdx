# Metrics

Metrics are the canonical, normalized measurements that form the core of ModularIoT's data model.

## What is a Metric?

A metric is a standardized measurement with well-defined:

- **Name** - Canonical key identifying what is measured
- **Value** - The measurement itself
- **Unit** - Standard unit of measurement
- **Timestamp** - When the measurement was taken
- **Source** - Which device or asset produced it

## Why Canonical Metrics?

Devices from different manufacturers report similar data differently. Canonical metrics:

- Enable cross-device analysis
- Power consistent symptom detection
- Simplify integration development
- Support historical comparison

## Metric Categories

### Position Metrics
Location, speed, heading, altitude

### Vehicle Metrics
Fuel level, odometer, engine RPM, coolant temperature

### Environmental Metrics
Ambient temperature, humidity, pressure

### Operational Metrics
Ignition state, idle time, utilization
