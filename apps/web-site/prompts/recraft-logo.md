### Recraft Prompt — _Balanced Tech-Mascot Icon — Armadillo Edition_

> **Design an iconic mascot for our open-source IoT platform.** > **Goal:** playful enough to feel like a “pet,” yet still whispers “engineering precision,” with no obvious overlap with well-known tech mascots.

---

#### Concept

- **Creature:** a _geometric armadillo_—its shell formed by **four interlocking hexagonal plates**, each plate representing a “module.”
- **Pose:** ¾ view, nose slightly up as if sniffing data; the tail curves forward forming a subtle waveform.
- **Mood:** friendly, intelligent eyes; approachable but not childish.

---

#### Style & Colour

- **Visual language:** flat-vector, crisp 45° / 60° edges (hexagon motif), 1.5 px outline.
- **Palette (solid fills, no gradients):**

  - **Core**  Blue 500 `#0790FF` (head highlight) | Blue 700 `#0051FF` (outline)
  - **Energy** Orange 500 `#FF6A14` (tail wave)
  - **Logic**  Yellow 500 `#FFB300` (shell centre plate)
  - **Neutral** Gray 600 `#545E66` (minor shadows)

- Keep any other hues ≤ 15 % of total area.

---

#### Deliverables

- **Icon-only** (no wordmark).
- Legible at 32 px; export SVG with transparent background.
- Layer names: `hex-shell-1/2/3/4`, `head`, `tail-wave`, `outline`, `eye`.

---

**Tone check:** not a corporate shield, not a kiddie cartoon—think “engineering mascot you’d proudly sticker on a laptop.”
