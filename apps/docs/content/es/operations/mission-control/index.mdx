# Mission Control

Mission Control is the operational nerve center of ModularIoT, where operators monitor fleet status, respond to symptoms, and manage day-to-day operations.

## Overview

Mission Control provides the tools and workflows for effective fleet operations, from real-time monitoring to structured incident response.

## Core Components

### Control Tower
The primary operational dashboard showing fleet status, active symptoms, and key metrics at a glance.

### Symptom Worklists
Prioritized queues of symptoms requiring operator attention, organized by severity, age, and assignment.

### Prioritization
Algorithms and rules that determine which symptoms require immediate attention.

### Operator Actions
Standardized workflows for responding to different symptom types.

### Escalation
Automatic and manual procedures for escalating unresolved symptoms.

### Audit & Traceability
Complete audit trail of operator actions and symptom state changes.

## Getting Started

Begin with the **Control Tower** guide to understand the main interface, then explore **Symptom Worklists** for operational workflow details.
