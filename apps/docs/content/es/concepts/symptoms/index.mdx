# Symptoms

Symptoms are the core abstraction that transforms raw telemetry into actionable operational intelligence. Understanding symptoms is essential to leveraging ModularIoT's full capabilities.

## What is a Symptom?

A **symptom** represents a detected condition in your fleet that may require attention. Unlike simple alerts, symptoms are stateful, trackable, and designed for operational workflows.

## Key Characteristics

- **Stateful** - Symptoms have a lifecycle (detected, acknowledged, resolved)
- **Severity-aware** - Conditions can escalate or de-escalate over time
- **Actionable** - Each symptom maps to a potential operator response
- **Traceable** - Full audit trail from detection to resolution
